Frequently asked questions

Your plant-based journey, simplified

Curious about nutrition coaching or how to reach your goals? Find answers to the most common questions from our community below.

How does nutrition coaching work?

You’ll get personalized support, meal guidance, and regular check-ins to help you reach your goals. We’ll work together to create habits that fit your lifestyle, making healthy eating simple and enjoyable.

Do I need to be vegan to join?

Not at all! Whether you’re fully plant-based, vegetarian, or just exploring, you’re welcome here. The focus is on helping you feel your best with a nutrition plan that matches your values and needs.

What results can I expect?

Most clients lose 5kg or more in 90 days, but every journey is unique. You’ll gain confidence, energy, and a better understanding of how to nourish your body for lasting change.

Is this program suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! No prior nutrition knowledge is needed. We’ll start with the basics and build up, so you feel supported every step of the way.

How are sessions delivered?

All coaching is online, making it easy to fit into your schedule. You’ll have access to resources, messaging, and video calls for ongoing support.

Can I get a custom nutrition report?

Yes! Share your details and goals, and you’ll receive a tailored nutrition report with actionable steps to kickstart your progress.
